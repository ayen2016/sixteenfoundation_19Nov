We Fund School Resources

We provide essential resources and tools for schools to enhance their learning environment for their students.
The schools we have supported lack access to simple renovations that require improvement. This simple change makes such a big difference to the learning and general presentation of the school.
With the right changes, schools can undoubtedly create better learning environments, thus allowing their students to thrive without compromise. Your contribution makes a tangible difference in educational success.

Darul Irfan

In Ramadhan 2021, we supported Darul Irfan Hifzul Quran Madrasah in the Kutapulong refugee camp, Bangladesh, providing shelter, food, and education for displaced Rohingyan children. With your donations, the school repaired its roof, purchased desks, bookshelves, uniforms, and installed a clean water supply, ensuring the children can learn in a safe and nurturing environment.

Lote Tree Primary

With 92 students enrolled, Sixteen Foundation supported the development of the school playground under Headteacher Mariam Ashique’s guidance. Renovations included laying AstroTurf for safe play in all weather, rebuilding the partition wall for privacy and safety, and adding storage for new play equipment, enhancing both the physical and mental well-being of the children.
