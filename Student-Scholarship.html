Empowering Students to Achieve their Dreams

At Sixteen Foundation, education is at the forefront of our organisation. We support young students with financial aid to pursue their education. Your contribution potentially helps to cover the school’s fees and their resources for the duration of their course, or in its entirety.

Many talented students face financial barriers that prevent them from pursuing their chosen path. Whether it is low-income families in the UK or orphaned children in Bangladesh, by funding scholarships, you help students unlock their potential and achieve academic and career goals, allowing another child to dare to dream.
