We Build School

Every Contribution Supports Educational Programmes, Local Projects, and Outreach, with Transparent Reports Showing the Real Impact of Your Support

Building hope, one classroom at a time

At Sixteen Foundation, we partner with like-minded organisations to support in the build of small-scale school constructions. With your donations we have been able to work in remote locations in Africa and Bangladesh. The gift of education in such places, does not only benefit the children attending the schools, but the community in its entirety.

Access to education begins with access to a safe, welcoming environment. Our Build a School initiative focuses on constructing classrooms in remote villages across the world, ensuring every child has the opportunity to learn in a secure environment, free of the changing elements.

Your contribution literally supports the foundation of education and helps us to lay the groundwork for brighter futures.
