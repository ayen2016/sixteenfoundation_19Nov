FAQs

Every contribution helps fund educational programs, community support projects, and outreach activities. We provide transparent quarterly reports to show the impact of your support.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I donate to the foundation?

You can donate online via our Donation Page using secure payment options including credit/debit cards and PayPal.

2. Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, donations to our foundation are eligible for tax deductions. A receipt will be sent to your email after completing the donation.

3. Can I volunteer for your programs?

Absolutely! Visit our Volunteer Page to learn about current opportunities and how you can get involved in our initiatives.

4. How is my donation used?

Every donation directly supports our community programs, educational initiatives, and volunteer-led projects. Transparency reports are published quarterly.

5. Can I make a recurring donation?

Yes! You can set up recurring monthly or annual donations on our Donation Page to help sustain our ongoing programs.
