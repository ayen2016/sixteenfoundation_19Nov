We Fund Student Resources

From the bare necessities required within the classroom to desirable resources that enhance learning, we act collectively with the shared vision of every child’s access to education and its resources.

Empowering Students with Tools to Learn

Many students lack access to the basic learning materials. At Sixteen Foundation, we partner with causes that really need our help to support children with the resources they need, from textbooks in their own language to technology in day-to-day learning.

With the proper resources, students can fully engage in their learning and achieve their full potential. With your donation, you become a part of their educational journey.

Vigsa

Sixteen Foundation supported the school by renovating its playground. AstroTurf was installed to allow safe outdoor play in all weather, the partition wall was rebuilt for safety and privacy, and additional storage was added for new play equipment..

Apex Secondary

Apex Secondary Islamic School, the first separate boys and girls’ school in Redbridge, continues the progression from their primary school under Headteacher Meherun Hamid. Sixteen Foundation supported the development of their Science Department by providing furniture, gas burners, sinks, and electrical points, ensuring students benefit from an enhanced learning environment with every donation going directly to the school.

Darul Uloom Jobeda

Darul Uloom Jobeda Khatun Mohila Madrasa in Dhaka provides shelter and education for 70 female orphans aged 7–9. Donations have created a safe, nurturing environment and supplied essential educational resources, including textbooks and school supplies, empowering these girls to thrive academically and emotionally.

Darul Ihsan

Darul Ihsan in Satkania, Chittagong, provides care and education for 120 boys and girls from primary to sixth form. Donations funded IT and sports resources, including 15 new computers, monitors, and keyboards, giving students the chance to develop vital technological skills and enhancing their learning environment.

Al Rawda

In Summer 2021, Sixteen Foundation supported Al Rawda in London by collaborating on the publication of “Prophetic Wellbeing: 40 Hadith on Healthy Living.” The books were distributed to the community, added to the library for students, and received 5-star reviews. A special dedication honoring the parents of Sixteen Foundation was also included.
