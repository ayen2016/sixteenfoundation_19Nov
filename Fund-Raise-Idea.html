A–Z Fundraising Ideas

Explore creative and meaningful ways to contribute from small gestures to large-scale projects every action brings us closer to a better future.

Creative Ways to Give Back

Whether you want to start small or organise something big, here are some inspiring ideas to make a difference.

A - Afternoon Tea Fundraiser

Host a friendly tea party and invite donations for a cause that matters.

B — Birthday Giving

Dedicate your birthday to raise funds instead of receiving gifts.

C — Charity Walk or Cycle

Get active! Organize a sponsored walk or cycle for your favorite campaign.

D — Dress Down Day

Let your office or school wear casuals for a day — with a small donation.

E — Eco Challenge

Take up an eco-friendly challenge and raise funds for sustainability projects.

F — Food Drive

Collect and donate food items to support local families or shelters.

G — Games Night

Host a games evening with entry donations going towards a school project.

H — Helping Hands

Volunteer your time to clean, paint, or repair community spaces.

I — Iftar Sponsorship

Support our Ramadan campaign — Treat an Iftar and feed those in need.

J — Jummah Donation

Make a small weekly Jummah contribution to ongoing charity work.

K — Knowledge Drive

Donate books or organize learning sessions for children in underfunded schools.

L — Local Business Support

Partner with a business to donate a small percentage of sales for charity.

M — Monthly Donation

Set up a small recurring donation — even a little goes a long way.

N — Neighbourhood Clean-Up

Gather volunteers to beautify your community while promoting giving back.

O — Online Fundraiser

Start a digital campaign and share your story to raise awareness and funds.

P — Plant a Tree

Donate or plant trees in memory of loved ones to help the environment.

Q — Quiz Night

Organize a community quiz — fun, competitive, and all for a good cause!

R — Ramadan Giving

Support our Ramadan campaigns — Iftar, food packs, and community support.

S — School Renovation Support

Contribute to renovating schools and empowering future generations.

T — Treat an Iftar

Order special meals to share kindness and joy during Ramadan.

U — Uniform Fund

Help provide school uniforms for children from low-income families.

V — Volunteer Drive

Encourage friends and family to volunteer their time for good causes.

W — Water Well Project

Support building wells in water-scarce areas around the world.

X — eXercise Challenge

Run, walk, or cycle for charity goals and share your progress online.

Y — Youth Empowerment

Invest in education and training programs for young people.

Z — Zakat & Sadaqah

Fulfil your obligation by supporting those most in need through verified programs.
