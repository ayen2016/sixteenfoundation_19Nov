Sixteen Foundation Scholarships

With the help of our late parent’s inheritance, we have been able to set up a small scale scholarship scheme. This scheme has already supported 6 students in the UK and abroad who have been struggling with their fees. In some cases we are able to support fully, whilst in other cases we help to kickstart their educational journey.

We would like to share with you a case study from a learner who was experiencing some financial struggle and wanted support to kickstart her aspirations to begin an Aalimiyah Course from a college in Lancaster.

“During my studies at Jamea Al Khauthar, I enjoy Seerah thoroughly as I feel a spiritual boost in lessons and love learning about the life of the Prophet (Peace be upon him). I admire the way my teacher explains in detail about the subject. I also enjoy dhikr time which takes place before Isha Salah, where I can sit and reflect after a busy day of studies. I am deeply grateful for Sixteen Foundation’s support in this.”

— Student H.

We love to hear from our learners and see the progress they are making in their respective courses. This would have been very supported by our late parents and their desire to see young people prosper in their educational endeavours. We wish Student H the best of luck with the remainder of her course and may it be a means of Sadaqah Jaariyah for our late parents and everyone involved with Sixteen Foundation.
