Our Promise to You

At Sixteen Foundation, we operate under a 100% Donation Policy. This means that every single pound, dollar, or taka you donate is used solely for the charitable projects you intend to support.

Our administration, marketing, and operational costs are covered separately through dedicated supporters and corporate sponsors — not from your donations.

100% Goes to the Cause

We ensure that every donation you make is used directly to support humanitarian relief, education, food drives, and community development.

Funded by Sponsors

All administrative expenses — including staff, offices, and logistics — are funded through sponsor partnerships and dedicated donors who specifically support our operational costs.

Transparency & Accountability

We maintain clear financial reports and project updates to show exactly where your donation goes and how it impacts lives.

Global Impact

Your donations reach schools, hospitals, and families in need across multiple countries — bringing sustainable and measurable change.

Why 100% Matters

Our 100% donation model builds trust and ensures that your generosity has the maximum possible impact. Whether it’s rebuilding schools, feeding families, or empowering youth — you can give with full confidence knowing your contribution makes a real difference.
