Corvarha Primary School

In Summer 2022, we supported Corvarha Primary School in Laximbaur Bazar Sunamganj, Bangladesh.

Thanks to all our donors.
This campaign is now closed.

They continue to teach the current cohort of children in the village and the larger build under construction, will allow room for more children to join and have some form of education. With your generous donations, the existing building has been demolished and a temporary tin classroom has been set up for the children to study in whilst construction is under way. This has allowed the builders to set the foundations of the new construction. This comprises of three sizeable classrooms, an office room and hallway. The build will be completed with a sturdy coloured tinned roof which will provide great shelter for the children during the monsoon season. The land surrounding the build is vast, so play and learning can be intertwined and enjoyed by all the children in their new setting. Local labourers are all working together in his rebuild, so there is a real sense of community, progressing as one entity and looking forward to something beneficial for all involved.

Highlights

1. The school continues teaching its current students while a new, larger building is under construction to welcome more children.

2. Donations helped demolish the old structure and set up a temporary tin classroom for continued learning.

3. The new build includes three classrooms, an office, and a hallway, topped with a durable tin roof for monsoon protection.

4. Local labourers are contributing to the project, fostering a strong sense of community and shared progress.
