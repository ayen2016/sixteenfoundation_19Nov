Highlights from Our Epic Snowdon Climb

Climb for Change 2025

Date: August 2 | Venue: Wales | Organiser: Araf Gulam

Our epic return to our annual Snowdon climb was as successful and bigger than before. Some of our climbers reached the summit under record timings and other took their time casually enjoying the epic views from every angle.

From beginners to expert climbers, everyone was supported by the group lead and made sure they enjoyed the experience together. Our iconic Sixteen Foundation t-shirts were on full display, conjuring up many thoughtful discussions up to the summit and descending back with crowds of climbers. A huge thank you to all involved during this climb.
