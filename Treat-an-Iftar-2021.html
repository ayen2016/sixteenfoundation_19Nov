Treat-an-Iftar: Delicious Community Initiative Led by Basori Hussain

Treat an Iftar 2021

Date: April 13, 2021 | Venue: London, UK | Organiser: Basori Hussain

‘Treat-an-Iftar’ was led by Basori Hussain and her team in the London and Greater London area. The package included a Savoury Platter with peri-peri rice, noodles, samosas, pakoras and a free relish at a cost of £20, alongside an optional Sweet Tray at a cost of £10.

The team conducted trial sessions during the weekends, with some more customised orders being placed. To keep up to date with our orders, we chose to use Google Forms, providing us with a simple, yet effective strategy to organise order details and deliveries efficiently to a high standard.

Our Highlights
